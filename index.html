Tips

Grace's Crafting Tips

  • Recycle and upcycle materials – old magazines, jars, and fabric scraps are treasure!
  • Keep a dedicated space for crafting, even a small corner counts.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with colors and textures!
  • Document your process so you can share with others and improve your skills.
Materials

Favorite Sources for Materials

  • Local Art Stores: Support small businesses for unique paper and tools.
  • Online: Etsy and Amazon often have affordable supplies.
  • Reuse: I save packaging, ribbons, and interesting containers for future projects!
