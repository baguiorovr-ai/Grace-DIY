About Me

My name is Grace, and art has always been a big part of my life. By day I’m a graphic designer, but in my heart, I’m a maker, an experimenter, and an idea-sharer. I started this website to bring together crafters of all skill levels and celebrate handmade creativity!

Why Crafts?

DIY crafts help me relax, connect with friends, and decorate my world. I believe anyone can craft, and this site exists to guide you from your first project into a lifetime of creative fun. Let’s learn together!
